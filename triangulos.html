Triangles

The triangle is a geometric form which contains special connotations beyond others. It contains simbolic and religious values and is an energetic activator by itself. While it can be seen as the triad or the trinity, the star of David represents the descending forces of the sky in correspondence with the ascendants of the earth, the pyramid of knowledge. The hourglass is composed of two inverted triangles. In the geometry of nature the triangle is the form that can develop asymmetrical bodies. For me it is a mystical form.
