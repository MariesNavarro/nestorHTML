About

Néstor Quiñones

Multidisciplinary artist. Since the late eighties, he has maintained a definite and critical view on the establishment conventions and formulas of the Art world. He has developed projects which focus, not only artistic creation, but on healing the existing cultural policies of Mexico by creating a meta-language superior to the formula. In 1988, he and his bother created La Quiñonera, an emblematic space in the art scene of the eighties in Mexico City that he continues to co-direct until today along with Ileana Peña Cid. For his sintomatologistas project, he has worked with homeopathy, as a way to create explorations related to the experience, aesthetics and devices. The sintomatologistas project is linked to perception, education and projection of reality and has been used to research and experience a series of educational and social events. His work has always been linked to ethical and metaphysical questions, as well as the subjective knowledge related to different cultures.
