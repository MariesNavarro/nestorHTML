La Quiñonera

La Quiñonera is an emblematic space on the art scene of the eighties in Mexico City. The history of this space contains historical elements, born at a time (1986) when Mexico had a close traditional and parochial spirit and in which, as every civilization, gave birth to elements of questioning, criticism, evolution and transformation in the creative, political, aesthetic and social field. La Quiñonera can be placed as a link of two eras: before and after the entry of the North American Free Trade Agreement. Musicians, filmmakers, theater people, actors, writers, artists, gallery owners, critics, curators, artists elderly, among other creators, were part of the spirit that carried the space. Today, La Quiñonera is a cultural space that connects its collaborators with the community in which it is settled through an academic, cultural and social methodology.
